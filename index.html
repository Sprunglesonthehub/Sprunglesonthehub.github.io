Core Identity

My name is Natalie, and at 23, I am defined by my passion for technology and my journey of self-discovery. My past name, Johnathan, is a "deadname"—a part of a history that no longer reflects the authentic person I am today. I approach life with a love for who I have become and a passion for the open-source world.

The Sprungles Philosophy

"Sprungles" is the constant in a life of change. It's the collection of my entire journey and the unchanging core of my identity: a love for Linux, for self-expression, and for making the world better by building superior, open alternatives to restrictive standards.
