Welcome to the Dossier: Initializing Connection

Hello, and thank you for being here. It means a great deal to me that you've taken the time to explore this corner of the internet. This space is a direct reflection of who I am, and I'm glad to share it with you.

My name is Natalie, though many in the open-source community know me by my alias, Sprungles. I am a proud transgender female (MtF), and every project I build is an extension of my journey toward authenticity and self-expression.

My Philosophy

This dossier is more than just a portfolio; it's an open invitation to connect. I am driven by a few core principles:

  • A Deep Love for Linux & FOSS: I believe passionately in open, user-controlled systems. The freedom to understand, modify, and control the technology we use every day is the bedrock of my digital philosophy.
  • An Open and Collaborative Spirit: I thrive on building things with other passionate people. This is an inclusive space, accepting of all individuals. Great ideas become incredible projects when minds connect.
  • A Willingness to Engage: I am always open to connecting with genuine, open-minded individuals for a conversation, a project, or even just a video chat. If you have an idea or just want to talk tech, my door is open.

Thank you again for stopping by. I'm excited to share my work and my journey with you.

Stay curious,
- Natalie "Sprungles"
